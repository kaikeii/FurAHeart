Mission

To protect and support the unprivileged animal community in the Philippines through modern day solutions, and give them a chance to be seen and cared by those with tender hearts.

Vision

FurAHeart Foundation aims to provide support and assistance to stray animals and animal shelters through a platform that strives to:
- Facilitate donation drives and animal adoption on the digital website.
- Create connections between prospective adopters, donors, and animal shelters for an efficient adoption and donation process.
- Assist shelters in acquiring necessary supplies and resources for the animals' daily needs and care.
- Increase interactions using social media outlets and collaborations to increase public visibility.
- Prevent the surge in animal homelessness and unfunded shelters by increasing adoption and funding opportunities.
